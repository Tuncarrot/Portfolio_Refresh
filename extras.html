Turi - This video was created as the background info-graphic for the 4x4 challenge. This video was made with Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, Photoshop and some Blender. This was a fun video to make, I had the chance to introduce myself to motion tracking and video overlays. Fun Stuff.



Mrthn - Much like the previous video, this is was also created with Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Blender. This was a promotional video created for the Mrthn service found at Mrthn.dev, which was another website I made. The interesting twist on this video was that PowerPoint was also used. I've never had the opportunity to play around with PowerPoint's animation features and I'm glad to say it was very easy. Additionally, I had the chance to play around with green screens, and while it needs more work, it did introduce me to some new techniques.
