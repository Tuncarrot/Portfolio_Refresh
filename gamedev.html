Twitch Arena

 The purpose of this game was to integrate Twitch API into unity to create an interactive streaming experience. The host would stream the game on twitch, and viewers could interact with the game through the chat. There are two teams; the hero team and the monster team. When a viewer joins a team, they are granted gold that could be spent on purchasing items, buffs or monsters for their respective team.
