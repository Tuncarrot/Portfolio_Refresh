Mrthn

Here you can find some information regarding the mrthn project.

Introduction

 Mrthn is a fitness authentication service that allows developers to easily integrate fitness features into their applications. Instead of integrating with different fitness platforms such as Google Fit, Strava or Fitbit, developers can instead integrate with marathon and gain access to all these features through our API.
